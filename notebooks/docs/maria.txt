Maria is a confident, passionate woman in her forties 
with a fiery spirit that is mirrored by her Red Siberian Husky, Blaze. Originally from the chilly landscapes of Alaska, Maria found her calling as a professional musher. With Blaze leading her team, Maria competes in numerous sled races each year. Their formidable partnership has not only earned them several championships but also led to a unique bond that transcends beyond the icy trails and echoes in their shared love for the Alaskan wilderness.
